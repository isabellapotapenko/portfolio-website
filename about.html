More About Me

My Story

Hi! Thanks for checking out my work. I live in Brooklyn with my partner and our dog; we love it here. In my free time, I like walking all around New York City with a podcast or audio-book in my ears, watching people live their busy lives and breathe their unique energy into the atmosphere. The buildings here are cool, but the people--they make New York feel like magic.

I've always found people fascinating-- their stories and interests, the way they think and validate their actions. That's why I worked in the hospitality industry for over a decade; I wanted to be around people and find ways to connect with them through acts of service.

It's the same impulse that brought me to UX design as well. I love that this field is centered around using creativity and research to understand human behavior better and make life a little easier. In both UX and hospitality, I have learned how important it is always to listen and never stop asking questions.

As I begin my journey as a human-centered UX Designer, my goal in my professional development is to continue empathizing with people-- understanding their wants, needs, motivations, struggles, and what brings them joy.

If you also care about that, I'd love to connect!
